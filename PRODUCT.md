# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Content creators, newsletter editors, and digital publishers who curate, compose, and distribute editorial content. They work in a browser, often with multiple tabs open, and value efficiency, clarity, and craft.

## Product Purpose

Forge is a SaaS platform that streamlines the content workflow: discover and curate articles (Digest), compose and refine content with AI assistance (Compose), and assemble and distribute newsletters (Newsletters). Success means a writer can go from raw source material to a polished newsletter in a single session without leaving the tool.

## Brand Personality

Editorial, tactile, confident. The brand feels like a well-designed publication — not a generic SaaS dashboard. Three words: **Sharp. Warm. Intentional.**

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards with blue primary, white cards, and heavy sidebars
- "Enterprise" interfaces with crowded data tables and flat, utilitarian design
- AI-generated-looking sites with glassmorphism, gradient text, and uniform card grids
- Cookie-cutter project management tools

## Design Principles

1. **Editorial, not admin.** Every surface should feel like a publication workspace, not a back-office dashboard. Typography leads; layout breathes.
2. **Tactile confidence.** Buttons, toggles, and interactions reward the user with subtle feedback — hover states, transitions, purposeful motion. Nothing feels brittle.
3. **Clarity over cleverness.** The UI gets out of the way. Actions are discoverable, labels are plain language, and the hierarchy guides the eye without decoration.
4. **One tool, one flow.** The Digest → Compose → Newsletters pipeline is a single narrative. Transitions between these modes should feel natural, not like switching apps.
5. **Craft everywhere.** Empty states, loading skeletons, error messages — every moment is designed, not defaulted.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA target (4.5:1 body text, 3:1 large text)
- Reduced motion respected across all animations
- Color not used as the sole differentiator for status or actions
- Keyboard-navigable by default
